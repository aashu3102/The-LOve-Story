25 August The Day We Found Each Other

— ✦ —

On 25 August, what looked like an ordinary day quietly turned into the beginning of us. A simple request, a simple hello, and suddenly two strangers started becoming familiar. From Snapchat to Instagram on 28 August, small conversations slowly turned into longer ones. Reels were shared, questions were asked, childhood stories were exchanged, and somewhere between laughs and late-night talks, something deeper started growing..

September brought butterflies, first calls, soft jealousy, and the comfort of hearing each other’s voices. We learned each other’s worlds dreams, fears, responsibilities, and hidden scars. There were fights too, moments of ego and silence, but even in distance we kept finding our way back. Every storm only proved that walking away was harder than staying.

October gave us something real commitment. Not rushed, not temporary, but intentional. Promises were made with open eyes. From good morning calls to shared playlists, from nicknames to imagined dates, we built a little universe of our own. You became my peace, my comfort, my home and I became someone better because you believed in me..

There were tears, insecurities, doubts about the future, and nights where we almost lost our way. But love kept choosing us. December tested us, yet it also strengthened us. We learned that real love is not about perfection it’s about coming back after the chaos, holding each other tighter than before.

And now, from 25 August to 7 March, we stand here — not as strangers, not as something temporary but as two hearts that have grown through everything. Every call, every fight, every laugh, every promise led us here. What started as a small click became a story written in patience, passion, and something that feels beautifully real.

I love you, Shambhavi. ♥
